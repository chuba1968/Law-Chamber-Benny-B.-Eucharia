LODGEMENT OF WILL

Lodgement of Will refers to the process of depositing the Will with the Probate and in the jurisdiction where the testator/testatrix lived or died.

We will lodge your Will adequately & in record time, before the appropriate probate court, which has jurisdiction.

Requirements

Please choose the kind of Will you want to lodge. A detached Will, is any Will left by a Testator/Testatrix who is deceased.

While the Ordinary Will, is a Will left by a Testator/Testatrix, who is alive.
